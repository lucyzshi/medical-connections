About Discovery Rounds

Discovery Rounds © 2026 by Lucy Z Shi is licensed under CC BY-NC-SA 4.0

Discovery Rounds is a free medical trivia game designed for anyone with an interest in the history of medicine.

Players solve clues related to medical history, landmark discoveries, diagnoses, pharmacology, and pathology. Discovery Rounds aims to foster appreciation for the history of medicine, the medical humanities, and the stories behind discoveries that continue to shape modern clinical practice.
